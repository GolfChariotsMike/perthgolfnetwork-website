PERTH GOLF NETWORK

WHAT IS IT AND WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

About PGN

WA's Largest Social Golf Club

  • The Perth Golf Network is WA's largest social golf club offering competitive golf in a relaxed friendly atmosphere.
  • In 2025 — 49,104 rounds were played at over 1,600 competitions, 50+ special events & 4 tours!
  • We gave out $250,000 in competition winnings, 5,000+ NTP prizes & $75,000 in special event and tour prizes!
  • The Perth Golf Network provides access to a Golf Australia handicap (using Golf ID) and professionally run competitions for all Perth golfers.
  • Open to men and women it provides a stepping stone for golfers to move from casual to club golf with the benefit of low membership fees and a pay as you go structure.
  • You pay a yearly fee and then pay green fees on the competition day.
  • You get all the benefits of playing in a private club with more than 35 competitions played at 24 different courses every week.
  • A PGN coordinator will be onsite at most competition days to look after members before rounds.
  • Throughout the year PGN will also run 'Special Events' giving members the chance to play at WA's most exclusive courses including Lake Karrinyup CC, Mount Lawley, Royal Fremantle GC, Royal Perth GC, The Western Australian GC and many more wonderful courses.
  • Perth Golf Network has an online portal as a main source of information for members. All results and handicap updates will be posted on the evening of each event. The homepage contains a facebook feed which is updated regularly with new information. We also send out a weekly newsletter to let members know about club news, Golf Australia competitions, upcoming events, changes in playing dates and course information.
JOIN NOW
  • Online bookings are available for all daily competitions.
  • Online bookings can be made 14 days prior from 5am. You will receive a confirmation email once your registration is complete.
  • Online bookings are the quickest and most efficient way to register for our weekly competitions and Special Events.
  • You'll need to add points to your account ($1 = 1pt) and a competition fee of 10pts will be used to secure your spot. Guests can also be added with their spot secured following payment of the comp fee. If you have not paid and the booking times out you will lose your reservation.
  • You are able to cancel your booking anytime up to 12hrs before the comp and receive an instant refund into your points account.
  • When booking Special Events as well as Marangaroo & Carramar daily comps the green fee and competition fee must be paid at time of booking.
  • Stableford — The most popular format. Points are scored based on your handicap. 1 point for a bogey, 2 for par, 3 for birdie, 4 for eagle.
  • Stroke — Your total number of shots minus your handicap.
  • Par — Playing against the course. A win, loss or halve on each hole.
  • Ambrose — Team format. Each player hits a tee shot, the best ball is selected, and all players play from that spot.
  • All PGN competitions operate under Golf Australia rules.
  • Members must have a current Golf Australia Handicap to enter competitions.
  • Results are posted on the members portal the evening of each event.
  • The weekly newsletter is sent every Thursday with upcoming comp info and news.
  • For competition queries contact: info@perthgolfnetwork.com.au
  • Membership is for a 12-month period.
  • Green fees are paid on the day of competition.
  • Bookings can be cancelled up to 12 hours before tee time for a full refund.
  • PGN reserves the right to cancel or reschedule competitions due to weather or course conditions.
  • Members must conduct themselves in accordance with golf etiquette and club rules.
Competition Day

ON THE DAY

  • Please arrive at least 20 minutes prior to tee-off and find the PGN representative running the competition. Note, not all competitions have a PGN representative — check the info button on each event.
  • To ensure that all players get a chance to play, groups will be required to tee-off on time with no exceptions!
  • It is imperative that you contact the PGN staff member running the day or the proshop if you are running late or need to cancel. This will allow players searching for a game at late notice an opportunity to play in the competition.
