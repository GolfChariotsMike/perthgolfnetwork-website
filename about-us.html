Who We Are

Perth's Home of
Social Competition Golf

Perth Golf Network is WA's largest social golf club offering competitive golf in a relaxed friendly atmosphere. In 2025, 49,104 rounds were played at over 1,600 competitions, 50+ special events & 4 tours!

We gave out $250,000 in competition winnings, 5,000+ NTP prizes & $75,000 in special event and tour prizes!

Open to men and women — PGN provides a stepping stone for golfers moving from casual to club golf with low membership fees and a pay-as-you-go structure. Pay a yearly membership fee, then just pay the green fees on the day you play.

How It Works

How Online Bookings Work

Simple, fast and flexible — book your next competition round in minutes.

📅

Book 14 Days in Advance

Secure your spot from 5am up to 14 days before the competition. Confirmation email sent instantly upon booking.

💰

Points Account

Add points to your account ($1 = 1pt). 10 points secures your spot at most competitions — easy and flexible.

🔄

Free Cancellations

Cancel up to 12 hours before the comp for an instant full refund back to your points account. Zero risk.

⭐

Special Events

For Special Events and some premium courses, green fee + comp fee is paid in full at the time of booking.

Competition Day

On The Day

What to expect when you arrive at the course for a PGN competition.

1

Arrive Early

Arrive 20 minutes before your tee time and find the PGN representative on site. They'll get you checked in and ready to go.

2

Tee Off On Time

Tee off on time — no exceptions. Groups must be ready at their tee when called. This keeps the day running smoothly for everyone.

3

Running Late?

Contact PGN staff if you're running late so others can take your spot. Results are posted online the same evening.
