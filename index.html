Who We Are

Perth's Home of
Social Competition Golf

Perth Golf Network is WA's largest social golf club — offering competitive golf in a relaxed, friendly atmosphere that's open to everyone. Men and women, beginners and experienced players, all welcome.

Our pay-as-you-go structure means no locked-in commitments. Pay your yearly membership fee, then just pay the green fee on the day you play. Simple.

  • Official Golf Australia Handicap (Golf ID)
  • 35 competitions across 24 home courses weekly
  • Online bookings — book up to 14 days in advance
  • Professional coordinator on site at every competition
  • Special Events at WA's most elite courses
  • Online results & handicap updates same evening
